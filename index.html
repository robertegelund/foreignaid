Foreign Aid Kit

Welcome to Foreign Aid Kit. This kit is made with the purpose of making foreign aid transactions and foreign aid spending more transparent.

After choosing the preferred continent, the total foreign aid amount from Norway to that continent will show up. You might also click on each country to get details about aid specifisities.

When you click on a country, you will the aid amount that is transferred to it from Norway from 1962 to 2018. A graph visualizing the relative aid amount that is unspecified or unallocated will also appear. The information will change when you click on another country.
